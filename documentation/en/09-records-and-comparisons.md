# Records and Comparisons

## Segment goal

This segment interprets stored workout history.
It is the analytical layer of the application.

## Main routes

- `/records`
- `/comparisons`

## Records

- calculate best results from workout history
- support records based on weight, reps, and duration
- filter results

## Comparisons

- selected exercise history
- chart configuration
- fullscreen chart view
- compare sessions over time

## Components

- `components/records`
- `components/records/helpers`
- `components/comparisions`
- `components/comparisions/helpers`
